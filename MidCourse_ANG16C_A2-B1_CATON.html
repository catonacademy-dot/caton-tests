SECTION 3 Compréhension écrite 10 pts · 6 min

A Weekend in the City

Last weekend, Anna and her friends decided to visit the city centre. They had been planning the trip for weeks. On Saturday morning, they took the train because the traffic is always terrible on weekends. They arrived at eleven and immediately went to a famous market near the river.

Anna has been to the market twice before and she loves it. She bought some flowers and a handmade scarf. Her friend Tom, who has never been there, was very surprised by the variety of food and crafts. In the afternoon, they visited an art gallery that had just opened. Although the exhibition was modern and not everyone's taste, they all agreed it was interesting.

They ended the day with dinner at an Italian restaurant. Anna said it was one of the best meals she had ever had. They plan to go back next month.
