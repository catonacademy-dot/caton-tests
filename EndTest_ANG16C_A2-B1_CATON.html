SECTION 3 Compréhension écrite 10 pts · 10 min
Texte — "A New Job" · Questions 1–5

A New Job

Sophie has just started a new job at a small marketing company in the centre of town. She works from Monday to Friday, from 9 am to 5 pm. Her office is open plan, which means everyone sits together in one big room. At first, Sophie found it a bit noisy and difficult to concentrate, but now she enjoys being around her colleagues.

Her manager, Tom, is very helpful. He always explains things clearly and never gets angry when someone makes a mistake. Sophie thinks he is an excellent boss.

One thing Sophie doesn't like is the commute. It takes her nearly an hour to get to work by train. She is thinking about moving closer to the office next year.

Overall, Sophie is happy with her new job. She feels she is learning a lot and the salary is better than her previous position.
